Meet the Maker

Hi, I’m Lex! I’m a self-taught baker turned pastry pro with a serious love for buttery croissants, bold espresso, and anything with berries. What started as a weekend hobby in my tiny kitchen turned into a full-blown obsession with crafting sweets that bring people together.

After training at SweetStart Academy and winning my local bake-off, I knew this was more than a passion — it’s my calling. Now, I run a pop-up bakery in Portland, and I’m dreaming up even bigger things, like a mobile bakery truck and a cozy café of my own.

Every treat I make is filled with love, laughter, and a little bit of pink ✨
