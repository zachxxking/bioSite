About Lex

Lex is a passionate baker with a dream to spread happiness through warm, freshly baked treats. Whether it’s flaky croissants or gooey cinnamon rolls, every creation comes from the heart.

Accomplishments

  • 🏆 Winner of the 2024 Local Artisan Bake-Off
  • 🎓 Completed Professional Pastry Training at SweetStart Academy
  • 🍞 Opened a weekend pop-up bakery in downtown Portland
  • 🧁 Featured in "Top 10 Bakers to Watch" by Sweet Life Magazine

Goals

  • 🚚 Launch a mobile bakery truck to reach more customers
  • 📚 Publish a recipe book focused on comforting home bakes
  • 🌱 Create a line of gluten-free and vegan treats
  • 🏡 Open a cozy café where community and pastries come together
